The case originated in applications against Russia lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on the various dates indicated in the appended table.
The applicant was represented by Ms V. Bokareva, a lawyer practising in Strasbourg.
The Russian Government (“the Government”) were given notice of the applications.
The applicant, Mr Nikolay Nikolayevich Skrypnikov, submitted two application forms: no. 41785/17, on 29 May 2017, and no. 59707/17, on 2 August 2017.
In the first application, no. 41785/17, the applicant complained of the excessive length of his pre-trial detention. In that application and in application no. 59707/17 he also complained under Article 5 § 4 of the Convention about lack of a speedy review of the detention matters (for details see the appended table below).
