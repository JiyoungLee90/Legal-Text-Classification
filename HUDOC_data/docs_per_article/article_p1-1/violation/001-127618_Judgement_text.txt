The case originated in an application (no. 49198/10) against the Republic of Serbia lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Serbian national, Ms Stana Jovčova (“the applicant”), on 17 August 2010.
The Serbian Government (“the Government”) were represented by their Agent, Mr S. Carić.
On 2 November 2011 the application was communicated to the Government.
The applicant was born in 1955 and lives in Bosilegrad.
The applicant was employed by HKP Yumco a.d., a socially/State-owned company based in Vranje (hereinafter – “the debtor”).
On 12 February 2007 the Bosilegrad Municipal Court ruled in favour of the applicant and ordered the debtor to pay her:
(a)  salary arrears in the amount of 70,387.12 Serbian dinars (RSD), plus statutory interest; and
(b)  RSD 5,517 (approximately EUR 70) for her legal costs.
On 2 March 2007 the judgment became final.
On 13 February 2008 the applicant filed a request for the enforcement of the above judgment before the Vranje Municipal Court, proposing that it be carried out by means of a bank transfer.
On 14 February 2008 the court accepted the applicant’s request and issued an enforcement order.
On 16 April 2010 the applicant lodged a constitutional appeal.
On 26 December 2012 the Constitutional Court held that the applicant’s “right to trial within reasonable time” was violated and ordered the Vranje Municipal Court and National Bank of Serbia to enforce the final judgment as soon as possible.
On 28 April 2007 the Bosilegrad Municipal Court ruled in favour of the applicant and ordered the debtor to pay her:
(a)  salary arrears in the amount of RSD 78,712 (aproximatelly EUR 990), plus statutory interest;
(b) employee meal benefits (naknada za ishranu na radu) in the amount of RSD 51,715.68 (approximately EUR 650), plus statutory interest;
(b)  RSD 14,360 (approximately EUR 185) for her legal costs; and
(c)  the pension, disability, and health contributions due for the period 1 January 2004 to 8 February 2005.
On 13 December 2007 the judgment became final.
On 13 February 2008 the applicant filed a request for the enforcement of the above judgment before the Vranje Municipal Court, proposing that it be carried out by means of a bank transfer.
On 15 April 2008 the court accepted the applicant’s request and issued an enforcement order.
On 16 April 2010 the applicant lodged a constitutional appeal.
According to information in the file the case is still pending before the Constitutional Court
