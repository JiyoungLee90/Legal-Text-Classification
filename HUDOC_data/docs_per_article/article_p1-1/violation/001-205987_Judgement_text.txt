The case originated in an application against Serbia lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on 17 November 2016.
The applicants were represented by Ms M. Lapadat, a lawyer practising in Belgrade.
The Serbian Government (“the Government”) were given notice of the application.
The applicants’ details and information relevant to the application are set out in the appended table.
The applicants complained of the non-enforcement of domestic decisions given against a socially/State-owned company.
