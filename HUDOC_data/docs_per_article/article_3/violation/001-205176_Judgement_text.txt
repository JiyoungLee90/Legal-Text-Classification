The case originated in an application against Ukraine lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on 9 December 2013.
The applicant was represented by Mr D. Govor, a lawyer practising in the city of Vinnytsya, Ukraine.
Notice of the application was given to the Ukrainian Government (“the Government”).
On 19 March 2014 the Court applied an interim measure under Rule 39 of the Rules of Court and priority treatment was given to the case under Rule 41 of the Rules of Court. On 10 November 2014, the interim measure was lifted.
The applicant’s details and information relevant to the application are set out in the appended table.
The applicant alleged that he did not receive adequate medical care in detention.
