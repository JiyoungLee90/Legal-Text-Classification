The case originated in an application against Ukraine lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on 17 April 2018.
The applicant was represented by Mr O.A. Ignatov, a lawyer practising in the village of Nyzhni Sirogozy, Ukraine.
Notice of the application was given to the Ukrainian Government (“the Government”).
