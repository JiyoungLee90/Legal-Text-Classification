The case originated in applications against Russia lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on the various dates indicated in the appended table.
The applications were communicated to the Russian Government (“the Government”).
The applicant, Mr Aleksey Nikolayevich Mitryukov, is a Russian national born on 6 May 1979. He submitted the three applications listed in the appended table and complained of the inadequate conditions of his detention in two detention facilities where he had been detained consecutively during various periods. He also raised complaints under Article 13 of the Convention. The relevant details of the applications are set out in the appended table.
