The case originated in an application against Hungary lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on 3 September 2018.
The applicant was represented by Ms L. Horgos, a lawyer practising in Budapest.
Notice of the application was given to the Hungarian Government (“the Government”).

The applicant complained of the excessive length of civil proceedings. He also raised other complaints under the provisions of the Convention.
