The case originated in an application (no. 11219/02) against the  lodged with the Court on 16 July 2001 under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by Ms B. Trojańczyk, the applicant.

On 14 February 2006 the Court decided to give notice of the application to the Government. Applying Article 29 § 3 of the Convention, it decided to rule on the admissibility and merits of the application at the same time.
The applicant was born in 1951 and lives in Warszawa.

On 14 February 1996 the Warsaw Court of Appeal dismissed the appeal.
