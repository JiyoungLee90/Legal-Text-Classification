The case originated in an application (no. 25073/05) against the  lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Hungarian national, Mrs Lászlóné Schwartz (“the applicant”), on 11 May 2005.
The Hungarian Government (“the Government”) were represented by Mr L. Höltzl, Agent, Ministry of Justice and Law Enforcement.
On 4 February 2009 the President of the Second Section decided to give notice of the application to the Government. It was also decided to rule on the admissibility and merits of the application at the same time (Article 29 § 3).
The applicant was born in 1944 and lives in Pilisvörösvár.
On 13 July 1998 a Mr M. and other plaintiffs brought an action against the applicant and Mr M. Wieszt, with a view to terminating common ownership of a property. The Buda Surroundings District Court gave judgment on 27 October 1999.
