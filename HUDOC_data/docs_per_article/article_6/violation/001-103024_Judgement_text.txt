The case originated in an application (no. 23086/07) against the  lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Turkish national, Mr Fevzi Terzi (“the applicant”), on 30 May 2007.
The applicant was represented by Mr Ö. Tekeş and Mr H. Akçay, lawyers practising in Amasya. The Turkish Government (“the Government”) were represented by their Agent.
On 27 August 2009 the Court decided to give notice of the application to the Government. It was also decided to rule on the admissibility and merits of the application at the same time (former Article 29 § 3).
