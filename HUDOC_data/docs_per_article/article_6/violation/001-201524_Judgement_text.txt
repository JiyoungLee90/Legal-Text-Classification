The case originated in an application against Serbia lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on 22 October 2009.
The applicant was represented by Ms Župić, a lawyer practising in Novi Pazar.
The Serbian Government (“the Government”) were given notice of the application.
