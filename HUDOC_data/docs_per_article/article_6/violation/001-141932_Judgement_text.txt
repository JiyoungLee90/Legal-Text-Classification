The case originated in an application (no. 22434/08) against the Republic of Hungary lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Hungarian national, Mr Csaba Kulcsár (“the applicant”), on 15 May 2008.
The applicant was represented by Mr I. Barbalics, a lawyer practising in Budapest. The Hungarian Government (“the Government”) were represented by Mr Z. Tallódi, Agent, Ministry of Public Administration and Justice.
On 26 October 2012 the application was communicated to the Government.
The applicant was born in 1954 and lives in Debrecen.
The applicant was party as creditor to liquidation proceedings which started before the Hajdú-Bihar-County County Regional Court on 12 March 1999. The case was terminated by the Debrecen Court of Appeal on 24 October 2007 (service: 29 November 2007).
