The applicant was born in 1966 and lives in Istanbul. The applicant was represented by Ms N. Selçuk and Mr H.B. Selçuk, lawyers practising in Istanbul.
The Government were represented by their Agent.
The Government did not object to the examination of the application by a Committee.
The facts of the case, as submitted by the parties, may be summarised as follows.
On 12 January 2000 the applicant was arrested on suspicion of having committed certain offences falling within the jurisdiction of the State Security Courts.
