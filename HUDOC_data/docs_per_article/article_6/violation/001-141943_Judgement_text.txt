The case originated in an application (no. 48689/10) against the Republic of Hungary lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Hungarian national, Ms Klára Lambertné Bársony (“the applicant”), on 18 August 2010.
The Hungarian Government (“the Government”) were represented by Mr Z. Tallódi, Agent, Ministry of Public Administration and Justice.
On 6 March 2013 the application was communicated to the Government.
The applicant was born in 1955 and lives in Miskolc.
On 20 April 1998 the applicant brought an action seeking division of the property of her common-law partnership with Mr F.
At first instance, the Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén County Court heard the case; on appeal, the Debrecen Court of Appeal. The final judgment was given by the Supreme Court on 2 March 2010.
