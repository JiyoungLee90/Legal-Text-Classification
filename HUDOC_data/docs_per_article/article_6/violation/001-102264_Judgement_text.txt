On 8 June 2009 the President of the Fourth Section of the Court decided to give notice of the application to the Government. It was also decided to rule on the admissibility and merits of the application at the same time (Article 29 § 1).

Background to the proceedings
On 14 March 1990 the  (Naczelny Sąd Administracyjny) quashed the decisions of 15 March and 5 May 1989. It referred to the Supreme Court's resolution of 20 February 1986, which held that a transfer of a real estate to the State Treasury under a sale contract was to be considered an expropriation for the purposes of the restitution proceedings.
