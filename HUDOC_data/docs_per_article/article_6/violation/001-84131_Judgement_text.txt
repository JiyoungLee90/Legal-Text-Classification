The applicant was born in 1944 and lives in the town of  in the Voronezh Region.

By judgment of 5 April 2004, the  awarded the applicant RUB 4,973.57. The judgment became final on 15 April 2004. It appears that it remains without enforcement.
On the same day, the  awarded the applicant RUB 16,765.50 and adjusted his monthly allowance to RUB 5,664.84. On 15 April 2004 the judgment became final.
