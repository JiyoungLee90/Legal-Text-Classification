The case originated in an application (no. 4153/10) against the Republic of Hungary lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by two Hungarian nationals, Ms Laura Széchenyi and Ms Eszter Széchenyi (“the applicants”), on 14 January 2010.
The Hungarian Government (“the Government”) were represented by Mr Z. Tallódi, Agent, Ministry of Public Administration and Justice.
On 3 January 2012 the application was communicated to the Government.
The applicants were born in 1976 and 1977 respectively and live in Budapest.
On 28 June 2001 the applicants brought a real-estate action against their mother and grandmother. On 23 November 2006 a first-instance decision was given. On 12 June 2007 the appeal judgment was adopted. In November 2009 the Supreme Court’s decision on the applicants’ petition for review was served.
