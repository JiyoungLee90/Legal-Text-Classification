The case originated in an application (no. 20518/04) against the  lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Russian national, Mr Sergey Leonidovich Tarasov (“the applicant”), on 23 April 2004.
The applicant was represented before the Court by
The applicant complained, under Article 6 of the Convention and Article 1 of Protocol No. 1, about continued non-enforcement of a judgment in his favour.
On 28 February 2005 the Court decided to give notice of the application to the Government. Under the provisions of Article 29 § 3 of the Convention, it decided to examine the merits of the application at the same time as its admissibility.
The applicant was born in 1959 and lives in the Perm Region.
In February 2003 the applicant, a retired military officer, sued the Military Department of the Orenburg Region for an increase of his pension and compensation for pecuniary damage.
On 5 March 2003 the Justice of the Peace of the  Circuit of the Totskiy District of the Orenburg Region allowed the applicant's action and awarded him 20,989.90 Russian roubles (RUR, approximately 608 euros). The judgment was not appealed against.
On 25 March 2003 the bailiffs' service opened enforcement proceedings.
On 18 July 2003 the bailiffs' service discontinued the enforcement proceedings because the debtor had no available funds. It advised the applicant to apply to the  regional department of the Ministry of Finance.
By letter of 4 September 2003, the  regional department advised the applicant to apply to head office of the Ministry of Finance.
On 6 January 2004 the Ministry of Finance refused to pay the award and referred the applicant back to the bailiffs' service.
On 1 April 2005 the Military Department transferred the amount due into the applicant's bank account.
On 16 May 2005 a prosecutor decided not to institute criminal proceedings against the head of the Military Department for his failure to enforce the judgment, finding that he had not acted with intent.
