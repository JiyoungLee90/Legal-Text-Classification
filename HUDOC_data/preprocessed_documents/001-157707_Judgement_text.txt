The case originated in an application (no. 60214/13) against Hungary lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Hungarian national, Mr Béla Urbán (“the applicant”), on 17 September 2013.
The applicant was represented by Mr A. Karakas, a lawyer practising in Szeghalom. The Hungarian Government (“the Government”) were represented by Mr Z. Tallódi, Agent, Ministry of Justice.
On 8 January 2015 the complaint concerning the length of the proceedings was communicated to the Government and the remainder of the application was declared inadmissible.
The applicant was born in 1940 and lives in Zsámbék.
The applicant lodged a compensation claim for medical negligence against a hospital on 10 October 2006.
The Pest Central District Court dismissed the action.
On appeal, the Budapest Regional Court upheld the firstinstance decision on 29 November 2011.
The applicant lodged a petition for review. The Kúria upheld the decision on 13 March 2013. The judgment was served on the applicant on 30 May 2013.
