The case originated in an application against Hungary lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Hungarian national, Mr Sándor Nagy (“the applicant”) on 18 June 2012.
The application was communicated to the Hungarian Government (“the Government”).
The applicant was born in 1967 and lives in Budapest.
He complained of the excessive length of civil proceedings which lasted six years and four months (between 26 November 2007 and 2 April 2014) for two levels of jurisdiction.
