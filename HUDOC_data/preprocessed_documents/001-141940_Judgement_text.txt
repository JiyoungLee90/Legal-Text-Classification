The case originated in an application (no. 36124/10) against the Republic of Hungary lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Hungarian national, Mr Faisal Abbas (“the applicant”), on 23 June 2010.
The Hungarian Government (“the Government”) were represented by Mr Z. Tallódi, Agent, Ministry of Public Administration and Justice.
On 6 March 2013 the application was communicated to the Government.
The applicant was born in 1959 and lives in Budapest.
The applicant was party to civil proceedings concerning the division of matrimonial property. The litigation started on 14 February 2004 before the Budapest IV/XV District Court, continued before the Budapest Regional Court, and was terminated by the Supreme Court on 9 March 2010.
