The case originated in an application against Montenegro lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on 3 May 2013.
The applicants were represented by Ms M. Camović, a lawyer practising in Podgorica.
The Montenegrin Government (“the Government”) were given notice of the application.
The list of applicants and the relevant details of the application are set out in the appended table.
The applicants complained of the excessive length of civil proceedings.
