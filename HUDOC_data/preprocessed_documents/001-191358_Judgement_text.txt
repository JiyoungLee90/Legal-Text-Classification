The case originated in an application against Ukraine lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on 25 June 2008.
The applicant was represented by Mr I. F. Tkach, a lawyer practising in Sevastopol.
Notice of the application was given to the Ukrainian Government (“the Government”).
