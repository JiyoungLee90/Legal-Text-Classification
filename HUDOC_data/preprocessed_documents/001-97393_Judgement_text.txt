The case originated in an application (no. 35187/04) against  lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Ukrainian national, Mr Ivan Mykhaylovych Mykulyn (“the applicant”), on 20 September 2004.

On 12 January 2009 the President of the Fifth Section decided to give notice of the application to the Government. It was also decided to rule on the admissibility and merits of the application at the same time (Article 29 § 3).

On 29 June 2005, following an order of the Ivano-Frankivsk Regional Court of Appeal, the case was transferred to the .
On 8 April 2008 the  partly allowed the applicant's and Mr L.'s claims.
On 6 May 2008 the  gave an additional judgment, ordering the applicant to reimburse legal expenses to Mr L.
