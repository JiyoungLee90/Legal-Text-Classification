The case originated in an application against Ukraine lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on 15 August 2014.
The applicant was represented by Ms A.N. Skalko, a lawyer practising in the city of Mykolayiv, Ukraine and Mr G.V. Tokarev, a lawyer practising in the city of Kharkiv, Ukraine.
Notice of the application was given to the Ukrainian Government (“the Government”).
On 19 August 2014 the Court applied an interim measure under Rule 39 of the Rules of Court and priority treatment was given to the case under Rule 41 of the Rules of Court. On 10 November 2016, the interim measure was lifted.

The applicant alleged that he did not receive adequate medical care in detention.
