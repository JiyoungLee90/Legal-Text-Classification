The case originated in an application against Ukraine lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on 21 January 2019.
The applicant was represented by Mr S.M. Rybiy, a lawyer practising in Dnipro.
The Ukrainian Government (“the Government”) were given notice of the application.
The applicant’s details and information relevant to the application are set out in the appended table.
The applicant complained of the excessive length of his pre-trial detention. The applicant also raised other complaints under the provisions of the Convention.
