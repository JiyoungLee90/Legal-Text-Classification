The case originated in an application against Hungary lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Hungarian national, Mr József Balogh, on 10 December 2012.
The application was communicated to the Hungarian Government (“the Government”).
The applicant was born in 1939 and lives in Remeteszőlős.
Between 31 August 2002 and 16 April 2013 – that is, for ten years and eight months – his action for division of matrimonial property and related issues was pending before the Budapest XX/XXI/XXIII District Court, the Budapest High Court and the Kúria.
He complained of the excessive length of the proceedings.
