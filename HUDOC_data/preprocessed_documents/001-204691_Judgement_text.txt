The case originated in an application against Romania lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) on 18 March 2015.
The applicant was represented by Ms N.V. Beleuță, a lawyer practising in Bucharest.
The Romanian Government (“the Government”) were given notice of the application.

The applicant complained of the excessive length of criminal proceedings and of the lack of any effective remedy in domestic law.
