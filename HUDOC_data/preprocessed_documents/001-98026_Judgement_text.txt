The case originated in an application (no. 25382/04) against the Republic of Bulgaria lodged with the Court on 24 June 2004 under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by two Bulgarian nationals, Mr Rangel Vulchev Stefanov and Mr Mitko Zdravkov Yurukov, who were born in 1972 and 1971 respectively and live in Plovdiv.
The applicants were represented by Mrs S. H. Stefanova and Mr A. Atanasov, lawyers practising in . The Bulgarian Government (“the Government”) were represented by their Agent, Ms M. Dimova, of the Ministry of Justice.
On 19 May 2008 the President of the Fifth Section decided to give notice of the application to the Government. It was also decided to rule on the admissibility and merits of the application at the same time (Article 29 § 3).
