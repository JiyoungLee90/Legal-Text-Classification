The case originated in an application (no. 17839/07) against the Republic of Turkey lodged with the Court under Article 34 of the Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms (“the Convention”) by a Turkish national, Mr Şenol Zaman (“the applicant”), on 18 April 2007.
The Turkish Government (“the Government”) were represented by their Agent.
On 27 August 2009 the President of the Second Section decided to give notice of the application to the Government. It was also decided to rule on the admissibility and merits of the application at the same time (former Article 29 § 3).

On 29 September 2004 the court dismissed the applicant's request.
