The present case concerns alleged ill-treatment of the applicants by police and the allegedly ineffective investigation of their respective complaints. In addition, it concerns an allegation by one of the applicants that the conditions of his detention were inhuman and degrading and that he had no effective remedies for this complaint.
The applicants’ details and the relevant facts are set out in the appended tables.
The Government were represented by their Agent, Mr I. Lishchyna.
